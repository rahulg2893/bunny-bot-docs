Bunny Bot — Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 2025

1. Introduction

Bunny Bot (“we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and your choices regarding your data when you use Bunny Bot.

2. Information We Collect

Bunny Bot collects minimal data required to function, including:

  • Discord user IDs and server IDs (to link actions to users/servers).
  • Server settings configured by admins (e.g., moderation settings, logs, prefixes).
  • Optional data you provide (such as reminders, starboard posts, or custom configurations).

We do not collect: passwords, direct messages, or personal identifying information outside Discord.

3. How We Use Information

We use collected data only to provide core bot features, such as:

  • Moderation (logging actions, applying punishments).
  • Utility features (reminders, autoresponders, stats).
  • Customization (server-specific settings).

Data is never sold or shared with third parties.

4. Data Storage & Retention

All stored data is kept in secure databases. Data is retained only as long as necessary for the bot’s functionality. If you remove the bot from your server, your server’s data may be automatically purged after a reasonable period.

5. Your Choices

  • Admins may reset or delete bot data for their server using available commands.
  • You may remove the bot from your server at any time to stop all data collection.

6. Third-Party Services

Bunny Bot interacts with the Discord API and may use external libraries (e.g., yt-dlp for music). These services are subject to their own privacy policies.

7. Children’s Privacy

Bunny Bot is not intended for children under 13. If you are under 13, you may not use the bot.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted here with a new “Last Updated” date.

9. Contact

If you have questions, concerns, or data deletion requests, contact us via our support server: Join Support Server.
